Job Opportunities

Join our team as a Player Recruiter for Esports India Cup. Your primary responsibility will be to recruit 10 players daily to participate in our tournaments. This is a target-based role with the following conditions:

  • Daily Target: Recruit 10 players to join tournaments.
  • Holidays: Sundays are holidays.
  • Salary: Rs. 50,000 per month.
  • Timings: There are no fixed timings. You can complete the given task in one hour or throughout the whole day. What matters is meeting the target. If you manage to complete the monthly target in just 10 days, you will still be paid for the full month. The pace is up to you.
  • Conditions: Salary is paid only if the daily or monthly target is consistently met. Failure to recruit players will result in no payment for that period.

If you are passionate about gaming and have strong networking skills, this is your opportunity to be part of India's growing eSports legacy. Apply now and help us build a vibrant gaming community!
