Rules

  • Gameplay Recording: All participants are required to record their gameplay throughout the tournament. Failure to provide footage may result in disqualification.
  • Fair Play: No use of cheats, hacks, or any third-party software that gives unfair advantages. Participants caught violating this rule will be permanently banned from the website and all future tournaments.
  • Behavior: Respectful and sportsmanlike behavior is expected at all times. Abusive language, trolling, or harassment will not be tolerated. Any violation may result in disqualification or a ban.
  • Team Composition: Ensure your team has the required number of players as per the tournament format. Substitutes are not allowed after the tournament has started unless otherwise specified.
  • Match Results: All match results must be submitted on time as instructed by the tournament organizers. Any discrepancies will be reviewed, and a decision will be made accordingly.
  • Server Selection: Players must join the designated servers provided by the organizers. No server hopping is allowed unless explicitly stated.
  • Equipment and Internet: Players are responsible for their equipment, including a stable internet connection. Lag or connectivity issues are not grounds for a re-match unless both teams agree.
  • Cheating Reports: If you believe another player is cheating or violating the rules, report it to the admins with relevant evidence (gameplay recording, screenshots, etc.). Reports without evidence may not be considered.
  • Eligibility: Participants must be 13 years or older to compete in our tournaments. Players must adhere to all local gaming laws.
  • Prize Distribution: Prizes will be awarded as per the tournament rules. No prize transfers will be allowed.
